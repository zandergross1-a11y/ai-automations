Short-Term Pain Relief (Before an Appointment)

Patients may use over-the-counter ibuprofen or acetaminophen for temporary relief unless they have an allergy or medical restriction.
This is not a substitute for an exam, but it can help manage discomfort until the doctor evaluates the tooth.
